Frequently Asked Questions

What is a temporary email?
A temporary email, also known as disposable email or throwaway mail, is a high-security email address designed to protect your primary inbox from spam, phishing, and data leaks. It allows you to receive verification codes and sign up for services anonymously without revealing your real identity.
How long do emails last?
To ensure maximum privacy, our system enforces a strict data retention policy. All received emails are securely stored for exactly 3 days before being permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers. Cleanup is automatic.
Can I use my own domain?
Absolutely! CleanTempMail offers advanced custom domain support. Simply configure your domain's MX records to point to our secure servers, and you can instantly generate unlimited disposable email addresses using your own branded domain name.
Is it free?
Yes, CleanTempMail is a 100% free temporary email service committed to internet privacy. We provide unlimited email generation and receiving capabilities at no cost, supported by non-intrusive advertising.
Common Use Cases?
Perfect for QA software testing, verifying social media accounts (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram), signing up for temporary services, and protecting your primary inbox from spam lists. Developers can also use our API for automated testing workflows.
Is my privacy truly protected?
Absolutely. We take privacy very seriously. No registration is required, we don't track your activity, and all emails are automatically deleted after the retention period. We don't sell or share any data with third parties. Your IP address is not logged, and we use HTTPS encryption for all connections.
Can I send emails from my temporary address?
CleanTempMail is designed for receiving emails only. This is intentional to prevent misuse and spam. If you need to send emails, we recommend using your primary email provider. Our service focuses on protecting your inbox when signing up for services or receiving verification codes.
Do you have an API for developers?
Yes! We offer a comprehensive REST API that allows developers to programmatically generate temporary emails, retrieve messages, and manage inboxes. The API is free to use with generous rate limits and includes detailed documentation with examples in multiple programming languages.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes, CleanTempMail is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your home screen for quick access, just like a native app but without the download from app stores.
Why are some emails not arriving?
Some websites may block known temporary email domains. If you experience delivery issues, try using a different domain from our extensive pool or use the custom domain feature with your own domain. Also ensure you're checking the correct inbox address and refresh the page.
What's the difference between temporary and regular email?
Regular email is tied to your identity and stores messages indefinitely, making it vulnerable to spam and data breaches. Temporary email is anonymous, requires no personal information, and automatically deletes all data after a set period, giving you privacy and a clean inbox.
Can I recover deleted emails?
No, once emails are deleted (either manually or automatically after the retention period), they are permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers. This is by design to ensure your privacy. If you need to keep important information, save it before it's deleted.
Is it safe for receiving verification codes?
Yes, it's one of the most common use cases. CleanTempMail is perfect for receiving verification codes, password reset links, and confirmation emails from websites. Just remember that anyone with your temporary address can see incoming emails, so don't use it for sensitive accounts.
How many email addresses can I create?
There's no limit! You can generate as many temporary email addresses as you need, completely free. Each address works independently, and you can switch between different domains to maximize deliverability and avoid potential blocks from certain websites.
What makes CleanTempMail different from other services?
CleanTempMail stands out with its massive domain pool (more than most paid services), free API access, custom domain support, real-time email arrival, and a commitment to privacy. We offer features that competitors charge for, all completely free and with no registration required.
